Privacy Policy for Private Home Automation SMS Bot

This SMS service is a private, family-only home automation bot used to send commands to a personal Control4 home system.

Information collected:
We store authorized family members’ phone numbers so the bot can verify who is allowed to use it. We may temporarily log incoming command text, timestamps,
and delivery/error information for troubleshooting and security.

How information is used:
Phone numbers and message content are used only to authenticate users, process requested home automation commands, and send confirmation or error replies.

Sharing:
We do not sell, rent, or share personal information for marketing. SMS messages are processed through Twilio, our messaging provider, and may be handled
according to Twilio’s own policies.

Opt out:
Authorized users may stop using the bot at any time by texting STOP or by asking the owner to remove their number from the allowlist.

Contact:
For questions or removal requests, contact the owner of this private home automation system.
